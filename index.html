About Project

Project Rupaantar is an initiative under Enactus MPSTME that focuses on transforming waste into valuable, marketable products. The project aims to create a circular economy by converting discarded materials into eco-friendly goods through innovative upcycling methods.

It not only promotes sustainable living but also empowers local communities by generating livelihood opportunities through manufacturing and sales. Rupaantar combines environmental responsibility with entrepreneurship, driving positive social and ecological impact while encouraging youth-led innovation in waste management.

Achievements

~Waste to Wealth~

DM Chiplun Meet

Presented our waste-to-wealth initiative at the District Magistrate Chiplun meet, showcasing how community-led upcycling creates sustainable livelihoods while reducing environmental impact. The project was well-received for its scalable model and grassroots approach to circular economy.

🌿

Enactus Regional Competition

Competed at the Enactus Regional Stage, demonstrating measurable social and ecological impact. Our team presented the transformation of over 165 kg of discarded materials into marketable eco-friendly products, empowering 20+ beneficiaries with increased incomes.

🏆

Community Outreach Program

Conducted workshops with local communities on innovative upcycling techniques, turning everyday waste into craft products. The program generated both awareness and income opportunities, directly impacting over 20 families and completing more than 90 product sales.
